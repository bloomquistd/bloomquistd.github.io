The above data is reported by the U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services. Each quarter reports on the data from the previous quarter. I.e.,
Q4 of 2023 reports on forms collected from July 1st, 2023 to September 30th, 2024.

Forms are organized by categories:

  • Family Based
  • Employment Based
  • Humanitarian
  • Lawful Permanent Residence
  • Citizenship and Nationality
  • Other
