Fear, Policy, and Paperwork: A Summer of Immigration Uncertainty

Due to the lagging nature of the data, it's difficult to conclude if the Trump Administration's policies are
having an impact on the volume of USCIS forms received. The most recently reported data only encompasses
2 months of the administration.

However, the data indicates that the federal layoffs have exacerbated the situation.

In July, the Trump administration opened "Alligator Alcatraz" in Florida, and announced they would be making
changes to the H-1B program. Coupled with a summer filled with shocking videos of ICE deportation efforts, we will have to wait
and see if these policies will become a deterrent to those seeking their own American dream.
